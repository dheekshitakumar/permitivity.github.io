Digitize, manage, and automate
the permit application experience with AI.

We use augmented and artifical intelligence (AI) to build digital connections between building regulations and the permitting process.

Digital Regulations Management System

Manage the full life-cycle of regulations, from creation to approval to revisions to removals. Not only that, our unique way of creating digital regulations allows us to directly drive workflows in the permit application process.

Intelligent Permit Application Generation

Use digital regulations to directly generate an intelligent, dynamic permit intake application that ensures all the information necessary to determine compliance with the regulations is collected from the applicant. Say goodbye to incomplete or incorrect applications!

Digital Pre-Approval Package

The digital pre-approval package significantly cuts the time to do a manual permit review. Given an application ID number, it displays the applicable regulations, displays results of the auto-checked regulations, and brings to the forefront any relevant information for regulations checked manually.

APIs for Review Workflows

Based on questions asked in the intelligent permit application form, we can determine which applications require specific reviews and fees. In other words, we expose responses to permit applications, to allow your city to directly drive permit review workflows based on permit application responses.

Cities / Municipalities
