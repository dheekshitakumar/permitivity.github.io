Our Product has 3 key components

Digital Regulations Management System

Manage the full life-cycle of regulations, from creation to approval to revisions to removals. Not only that, our unique way of creating digital regulations allows us to directly drive workflows in the permit application process.

Key capabilities:

  • Auto-checks all regulations de
  • Smart-filter of regulations by project features
  • View the history of each regulation

Dynamic Permit Intake Form

Use digital regulations to directly auto-generate an intelligent, dynamic permit intake application that ensures all the information necessary to determine compliance with the regulations is collected from the applicant. Say goodbye to incomplete or incorrect applications!

Key capabilities:

  • Only shows applicant relevant questions based on previous answers
  • Questions are directly linked to each regulation for full-transparency
  • Provides live feedback on compliance with regulations

Auto-checked Permit Application

Our software can automatically check and issue a permit. In cases where a manual review is required, we automatically generate a digital pre-approval package that significantly cuts the time to do a manual permit review.

Key capabilities:

  • Calculate and display all applicable regulations
  • Display results of all autochecked regulations
  • Provide easy UI to search and parse permit application material

Our Mission

Our mission at Permitivity is to bring down the cost of residential development and enable affordable housing and new sustainability efforts by simplifying the permitting process. Getting a permit is a complicated, expensive, and time-consuming process that can dramatically increase a project's cost and time to completion. Our goal is to reduce the time and cost associated with the permitting process so that we can accelerate the development of the housing and sustainability infrastructure we so desperately need.
